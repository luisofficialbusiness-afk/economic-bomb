Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026  ·  Economic Bomb
Economic Bomb is a Discord economy bot. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

What We Collect

Economic Bomb collects and stores the following data to operate the bot's economy system:

  • Your Discord user ID
  • The Discord server (guild) ID where you use the bot
  • Economy data: wallet balance, bank balance, stock portfolio holdings
  • Slave system data: ownership status, debt amounts, and earnings history
  • Command usage timestamps (for cooldown enforcement)

We do not collect your email address, real name, password, or any personal information beyond what Discord provides through its public API.

How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used solely to operate the bot's features:

  • Storing and retrieving your in-game economy balances
  • Tracking cooldowns to prevent command abuse
  • Enforcing server-specific economy settings configured by server admins
  • Displaying leaderboards and statistics within your server

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose outside of operating the bot.

Dashboard & OAuth2

If you use the Economic Bomb dashboard, we request access to the following Discord OAuth2 scopes:

  • identify — to verify your Discord identity and display your username
  • guilds — to determine which servers you manage that the bot is in

We do not store your OAuth2 access token beyond your active session. Session data is cleared when you log out or after 24 hours of inactivity.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in a MongoDB database. We take reasonable precautions to protect stored data, but cannot guarantee absolute security. Economy data is stored only as long as you or your server continues to use the bot.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us in our support server. Server admins can also reset all economy data for their server using the ?oresetleaderboard command.

When the bot is removed from a server, data associated with that server is not automatically deleted — contact us if you'd like it removed.

Third Parties

Economic Bomb uses the following third-party services:

  • Discord — the platform the bot operates on. Subject to Discord's own privacy policy.
  • MongoDB — for data storage.

We do not integrate with any advertising networks or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

Economic Bomb follows Discord's Terms of Service, which require users to be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Significant changes will be announced in our support server.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, please reach out through our support server.
