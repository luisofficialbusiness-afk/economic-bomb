💰 Economy 6 commands
?balance?bal
Check your wallet and bank balance.
?balance
?work5 min cooldown
Earn money. If enslaved, all earnings go to your owner and reduce your debt.
?work
Earns $25–$100 per use
Enslaved users: earnings route to owner automatically
?deposit?dep
Move cash from your wallet into your bank.
?deposit [amount] | all
?withdraw?with
Withdraw from your bank back into your wallet.
?withdraw [amount] | all
?givemoney?give
Transfer money from your wallet to another player.
?give @user [amount]
?rob10 min cooldownRisk
Attempt to steal from another player's wallet. Getting caught costs you money.
?rob @user
Target needs at least $50 in wallet
Base success: 60%, scales down with target's wealth
If caught: lose 15% of your balance (min $200)

⛓️ Slave System 5 commands
?buyHigh Stakes
Purchase another player. The price is 2× their current wallet balance. The target has 2 minutes to outbid you — if they don't, they're yours.
?buy @user
Buy price = target's balance × 2
Once bought, all their ?work earnings go to you
They must earn 2× the buy price to be freed
?outbid
Use within 2 minutes of a buy attempt to escape. You must offer more than the buy price.
?outbid [amount]
?slave
Check your slave status — who owns you, remaining debt, and total earned for your owner.
?slave
?slavepanel
Owner panel to manage your slaves. Shows stats with buttons to free, renew, or refresh.
?slavepanel
Set Free — release immediately at no cost
Renew — pay half their current debt to double it
Refresh — pull updated stats
?slavelist
Leaderboard of who owns the most slaves in the server.
?slavelist
